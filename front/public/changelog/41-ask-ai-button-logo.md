title: Ask AI button logo setting
date: 2026-02-13
type: changelog
tags: focus
---

You can now choose whether to show the logo on the Ask AI button you embed on your site. When enabled, the button displays the chat bubble icon above the "Ask AI" text. Toggle **Show logo** in the [**Customise widget**](https://crawlchat.app/connect#customise-widget) section.
